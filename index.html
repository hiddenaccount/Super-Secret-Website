What's New?

With the first arrival of Kyotokek in form of beta release, there's a few questions you all probably have regarding the website, I will try to answer all of them as best I can.

QUICK FAQ:
1) Kyotokek's website looks strangely similar to that of Night's website. Did you guys skid?
1a) I actually intended for Kyotokek to have some similarities to that of Night and dorcusKek. As you all know by now, the Night team no longer mods Kik. (At least officially.) With that being said, this layout was one of their older versions of their original website which I took a liking to, so I decided to modify it myself. However, this website is only a placeholder and we will be making a website that doesn't rely on using Night as a template in the future. My intentions were not to skid or disrespect the work Kat or anyone else on the Night team has done in the past, but instead to bring Night and the developers some light once again for the future to show just how much work they have done for the community as a whole.
TL;DR: Credit to Night Team, you guys are awesome.

2) Well, what's the difference from Night's website and Kyotokek's website?
2a) A lot of things were changed from the original website. For starters, a ton of the code was modified, we changed the background video, the devs section (obviously,) the colors, the logo, and some of the layout was also changed around to make sure it's not a carbon copy of that of Night, but also to make the site look better overall for our users.

3) I found a bug, how can I report it?
3a) The website still isn't quite finished yet and quirks are expected. If you happen to find any bugs or anything else, contact Kiyo privately. (Username under "Contact".)

4) Will there ever be any more updates of Kyotokek or any other projects? If so, will they be hosted on the same website?
4a) Yep! Future updates are on the way. :) All future updates will be hosted through www.kyotokek.ml until we change domains. As for other projects, we have a few things in mind. No promises, though.

5) Why does Kyotokek come up as unsafe when I go to download? And what do I do if I can't download at all?
 5a) No worries, Kyotokek is safe to use for all users. 90% of the time, this is due to your phone's default settings. You'll need to enable Developers Settings and Unknown Sources. First, open the Settings app. Depending on your device it could be something else, but normally it should be titled "Security." Go there. Once you find that, scroll down until you find something called "Unknown Sources." This allows you to install apps outside of the Play Store directly from your browser. After that's turned on you should be able to download Kyotokek. Though it is advised that after you download that you turn Unknown Sources back off.


If you have anymore questions regarding Kyotokek, questions or updates, feel free to join the official Kyotokek group on Kik. Just search for public groups and type in #Kyotokek. :)


-Kiyo
